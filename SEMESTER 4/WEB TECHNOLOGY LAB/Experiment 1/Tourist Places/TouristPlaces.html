Robber's Cave (Guchhupani)

A natural cave formation where a river flows inside, providing a unique experience.

Sahastradhara

Known for its breathtaking beauty, Sahastradhara is a collection of waterfalls and caves with therapeutic properties.

Tapkeshwar Temple

A sacred cave temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its natural Shivalinga and the dripping water from the rock.

Mindrolling Monastery

One of the largest Buddhist monasteries in India, known for its impressive architecture and serene surroundings.

Forest Research Institute (FRI)

A premier institution for forestry research, FRI is surrounded by lush greenery and colonial-era buildings.
